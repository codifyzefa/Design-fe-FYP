Features

Core tools for managing FYP work with less friction.

The portal brings project submission, supervision, progress tracking, evaluation, and reporting into one coordinated academic workspace.

Role Based Dashboards

Students, supervisors, evaluators, and coordinators each get focused workflows.

Central Records

Submissions, reviews, grades, and reports stay organized in the portal.

Digital Submissions

Submit proposals, reports, documents, and evidence through a structured online workflow.

Supervisor Coordination

Keep supervisor allocation, feedback, and project communication aligned throughout the cycle.

Progress Tracking

Monitor milestones, logs, completion status, and review readiness from one place.

Evaluation Management

Support evaluator assignment, scoring, remarks, and final assessment records.

Announcements

Publish deadlines, schedules, notices, and departmental updates for all FYP groups.

Reports

Organize project status, evaluation summaries, and administrative reporting in a cleaner format.
