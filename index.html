The Program Purpose

Empowering Students Through Applied Research

FYP Lifecycle

Understanding the FYP Process

01

Proposal Submission

Students submit project proposals with title, abstract, objectives, and methodology for review.

02

Supervisor Assignment

FYP coordinator assigns qualified supervisors based on domain expertise and availability.

03

Progress Tracking

Regular progress updates, milestone submissions, and supervisor feedback cycles throughout the year.

04

Final Evaluation

Comprehensive evaluation panel review, viva voce, and final grading by faculty committee.
